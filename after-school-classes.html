What is Maktab?

Islamic after-school classes, commonly known as Maktab, are supplementary educational programs designed to provide children and teenagers in Muslim communities with a deeper understanding of Islam. These classes typically take place outside of regular school hours, offering a dedicated space for spiritual and intellectual growth.

Our program focuses on foundational Islamic principles, including the Quran, Hadith, Islamic history, Fiqh (jurisprudence), and Aqidah (creed). We aim to equip students with the knowledge and skills necessary to navigate modern life while staying true to their faith.curriculum-gridcurriculum-grid

Our Engaging Curriculum

Unique & Standards-Based Books

Our curriculum utilizes new, unique books developed specifically for our program. These resources are crafted on the basis of high educational standards and the core focus of our program, ensuring a comprehensive and relevant learning experience.

The curriculum is designed to be engaging, challenging, and enriched, focusing on vital topics like Islamic ethics, jurisprudence, and spirituality. We strive to make learning enjoyable and impactful.

Holistic Development & National Exams

In some areas, our classes are designed to meet the requirements for national exams, covering major and minor subjects such as Math, Science, and English, alongside Islamic studies. This holistic approach supports students' overall academic success.

We believe in nurturing well-rounded individuals who excel not only in their religious knowledge but also in their secular education, preparing them for future challenges.

Dedicated Teachers

Our Islamic after-school classes are often taught by passionate volunteer teachers. These dedicated individuals are highly knowledgeable in Islamic studies and are committed to sharing their wisdom with the next generation. Their enthusiasm creates an inspiring learning environment.

Classes are held in various accessible locations, including local mosques, vibrant community centers, or other dedicated educational spaces. We ensure a comfortable and conducive environment for all our students.
